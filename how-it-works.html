How it works

From scattered information to clear next steps.

Voithos360 helps patients prepare before a visit, understand what happened, and follow through after the appointment.

Join early access Watch preview

Prepare for the visit

Voithos360 organizes what may matter, including age-based reminders, uploaded documents, symptoms, and questions to ask.

Understand results and conversations

Lab reports, imaging reports, and visit conversations are turned into patient-friendly summaries with practical next steps.

Follow through

Voithos360 helps create actions such as scheduling, repeat labs, specialist prep, caregiver updates, and advocacy requests.

Keep caregivers aligned

With patient approval, caregiver updates can be prepared and shared through a secure link.
