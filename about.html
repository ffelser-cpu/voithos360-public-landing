Built for the person trying to make sense of care.

Founder Bio

Felice Felser

Founder of Voithos360

Felice Felser is the founder of Voithos360, a patient-facing AI care navigation company focused on helping people understand what to ask, what to schedule, and what to do next. Drawing from experience as a Nurse Practitioner, product leader, and digital health strategist, Felice is building technology for the real-world moments where care becomes fragmented, confusing, and emotionally heavy.

Voithos360 is designed to support patients and caregivers before, during, and after care encounters, with an emphasis on clarity, follow-through, and human-centered support.

Why this matters

Healthcare asks patients to be project managers.

Voithos360 helps carry some of that load by turning visits, documents, referrals, and care questions into clear next steps people can actually use.
